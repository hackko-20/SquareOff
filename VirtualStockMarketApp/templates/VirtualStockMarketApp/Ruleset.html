RULESET

  • You will be given $ 1,000,000 at the moment of sign up. This will be your cash balance
  • Every day during market hours, you can make trades, buy and sell stocks. The market hours are from 7:00PM IST to 1:30PM IST
  • You can make intraday trades. All the transactions made during the day(intraday) should be squared off by the end of the day. The intraday trading limit is always 3 times your cash balance as present at the start of the market hours.
  • If your intraday transactions are not squared off by you at the end of the day, they will be squared off automatically (buying/selling stocks at market price)
    In case you do not have sufficient funds for such transactions, your profile will be automatically reset
  • You can track stocks of your choice by adding them to the favourites
  • When your Cash balance becomes zero, your profile will be automatically reset
